Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit

Year-round , but peak season is during the dry months from June to September

Consider visiting during weekdays for fewer crowds

Currency

Local currency is paradise Dollars(PD)

Major credit cards are widely accepted

Language

English and paradisean Creole are widely Spoken.

Learn a few local phrases to enhance your interaction with locals.

Local Cuisine

Indulge in the rich flavours of paradise Island's local cusine, featuring a fusion of seafood, tropical fruits and aromatic spices.

Weather Conditions

While the weather is generally sunny, occasional tropical showers can occur, It's advisable to carry a light raincoat or umbrella

Check the weather forecast before planning outdoor activities.
